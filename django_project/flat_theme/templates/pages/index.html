Welcome to the home page for the South African QGIS User Group.

Regional Meetups

Since South Africa is a large country, it is not convenient for most of our members to convene in a central location. Instead we are forming regional subgroups.

Cape Town

The Cape Town subgroup had their first meeting on November 10th 2016 at Chief Directorate: National Geo-spatial Information in Mowbray. Have a look at our blog post to see photos from this meeting

Gauteng

The first Gauteng meetup is due to be held at the CSIR in Pretoria on November 25th. Please look at our events calendar for details.

Your region?

If you are interested in holding meetups in another region and have sufficient local interest, please let us know and we will add the details here.
